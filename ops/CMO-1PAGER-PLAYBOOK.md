# CMO Playbook — How to Build a Through Air 1-Pager

> This document aligns the CMO on how Through Air creates client proposals.
> Every 1-pager must feel personal, outcome-driven, and easy to say yes to.

---

## The One Rule

**Never sell a service. Sell a result.**

Bad: "We manage your social media."
Good: "We get you 15% more followers and 3x more DMs from buyers — every month."

## How to Build a 1-Pager (Step by Step)

### Step 1 — Research the client (10 min)

Before opening any template, answer these 4 questions:

1. **What do they sell?** (product, service, ticket size)
2. **Where are they weak?** (bad social media, no website, no ads, slow response time)
3. **What result would change their business?** (more leads, more foot traffic, more sales)
4. **Who makes the decision?** (owner, CEO, marketing manager)

### Step 2 — Pick 2–3 services max

Do not list everything. Pick the services that directly fix their biggest problem.

| Client Type | Recommended Stack |
|---|---|
| Restaurant / Bar | Social Media + Paid Ads + Chatbot |
| Dental / Medical Clinic | Paid Ads + Chatbot + Automated Reports |
| Real Estate Agency | Paid Ads + AI-Native Pack |
| Boutique Hotel | Social Media + Paid Ads + Website |
| Fashion / Retail | Social Media + E-Commerce + Paid Ads |
| School / College | Website + Paid Ads + Automated Reports |
| Tech Startup | AI-Native Pack + SEO + Chatbot |

### Step 3 — Fill the 1-Pager template

Use the structure below. Keep it to one page. No fluff.

## How to Personalize (the 3 things you must change per client)

### 1. The Challenge line
Research their social media, Google reviews, and website. Find the real gap.
- No website → "operating entirely offline in a digital-first market"
- Bad reviews → "getting new patients but losing them before the second visit"
- No engagement → "posting 4x a week with no replies, no leads, no traction"

### 2. The ROI math
Ask the CEO for the average ticket size before sending.
Then do the math: `[leads/mo] × [ticket] = [monthly revenue] vs [our fee]`
The ratio should be at least 3x.

### 3. The Guarantee
Match the guarantee to their biggest fear:
- "We won't see results" → money-back or free month guarantee
- "It's too expensive" → performance-based payment option
- "We've tried agencies before" → 30-day cancel anytime clause

## What a Good 1-Pager Is NOT

- Not a PDF with 8 pages of case studies
- Not a price list with 12 services
- Not a generic deck with our logo and "we're the best"
- Not sent before the discovery call (always call first, then send)

---

## Delivery Process

1. **Discovery call** (15–20 min) — understand the client before writing anything
2. **Build the 1-pager** — takes 20–30 min max using this playbook
3. **Send same day** — while the conversation is still warm
4. **Follow up in 48h** — one message, no pressure: "Any questions on the proposal?"
5. **Close or learn** — if no, ask what stopped them. Update this playbook.

---

## Pricing Rules

- Never discount the monthly retainer
- Setup fees can be waived for high-value clients ($1,000+/mo) if needed to close
- Always anchor the price against the ROI, not against competitors
- Minimum engagement: $275/mo. Anything below is not worth the account management
