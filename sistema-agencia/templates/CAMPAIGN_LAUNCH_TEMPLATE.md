## Fase Pre-Lanzamiento (4 semanas antes)

### Semana 1: Planning
- [ ] Definir objetivo de campaña (conversión, awareness, engagement)
- [ ] Identificar target audience (segmentación)
- [ ] Determinar channels (email, social, ads, PR)
- [ ] Asignar responsables por canal
- [ ] Definir budget allocation
- [ ] Establecer KPIs y métricas de éxito

### Semana 2: Creative & Messaging
- [ ] Brainstorm de conceptos
- [ ] Crear messaging framework
- [ ] Desarrollar creative assets
- [ ] Escribir copy (email, ads, social)
- [ ] Diseñar graphics/videos
- [ ] Review interno y feedback

### Semana 3: Setup Técnico
- [ ] Crear landing page (si aplica)
- [ ] Setup email sequences
- [ ] Configurar tracking/pixels
- [ ] Programar posts en redes
- [ ] Setup de ads (audience, budget, targeting)
- [ ] QA y testing de todos los elementos

### Semana 4: Teasing & Anticipation
- [ ] Begin teasing en redes sociales
- [ ] Email sneak-peek a lista segmentada
- [ ] PR outreach (si aplica)
- [ ] Partner communications
- [ ] Internal communication
- [ ] Final QA checks
